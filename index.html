EVERYONE HAS 2 HOURS
It's not an exaggeration to say that America is in the midst of an existential crisis*. The only way to turn the tide is to prevent Donald Trump from being elected President to a second term. The reality is that DT will be re-elected unless people do something about it. And you know what's cool? WE are people! We have agency. And no one else will do it for us; it's on us. How many minutes a month do we read or listen to the news and feel terrible? What if we took even 5% of that time and replaced it with actually DOING something to stop this?

The truth is that while we’re all busy, literally EVERYONE can find 2 hours a month to save our democracy. Yes, including you! So, let's do something! Something doesn't have to be "everything," or even "a lot," but it has to be "something." Just once a month is all we're going for.

While there are many ways to be politically active, this tool is about 1 thing -- getting Trump out of the office, and on a blue wave that allows Democrats to flip the Senate.

This tool will help us hold ourselves accountable by making sure we do what we all know is so important -- donating our time and our money to saving America.

Groups of friends, families, acquaintances, whoever, can take this tool and use it to hold each other accountable. By signing up, every member of your group commits to donating their $ and their time once a month from now through November 2020. By the last day of the month, each team member will track their progress on their team's "Track My Progress" tracker for themselves and the group to see! Need ideas on where to donate and how to act? Check out the e-mail your captain will send to you each month, and the other tabs in this file --> -->.

Speaking of which, each group should have a team captain. The team captain is responsible for making things as easy as possible for members! And we will make it as easy as possible for captains! It takes less than 1 hour/month to be a team captain. If you are interested in becoming a team captain e-mail us for instructions: letsdosomething2020@gmail.com.

While traditional Democratic priorities like health care & the economy are important to many of us, this isn't about that. We are focused on existential threats that should not be political, like our President preaching blatant racism, his leadership in the erosion of democratic norms, and his support for destroying this planet by ignoring climate change.
